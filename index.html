Lake Victoria Athletic Club

Founded in 2011, LVAC is a grassroots organization committed to promoting recreational and competitive water sports as a means to boost healthy lifestyles, hope, productivity, community, and citizenship in the Lake Victoria Region.

We provide organizational, operational, and funding support to rowing clubs in Kenya. This includes facilitating elite and recreational programming, including programs for at-risk groups. We serve a diverse group of men and women across two clubs in Kisumu and Ogal Beach.

Rowing

Since its founding, LVAC has emerged as one of the premier rowing clubs in Kenya. LVAC athletes have performed at the highest levels nationally and aspire to contribute to Kenya's continued legacy of success in endurance sports on the international level.

Swimming

LVAC promotes swimming as a competitive activity as well as a critical skill for members of the Kisumu community to possess for safety reasons. Our coaches are skilled swimmers and instructors who have the tools to learn the fundamentals or advanced techniques.

Innovation

As athletes, rowers are challenged to maximize boatspeed through optimal power application and mechanics. As a club, our challenge is to achieve efficient, sustainable operations while incurring minimum costs. We draw inspiration globally, but invest locally.
